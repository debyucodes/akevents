About Us

AK Events Group’s singular aim is to create memorable and innovative events, while creating an impact in the community. AK Events Group offers event development, management and production services in order to create events that empower and engage the community.

engagement

AK Events Group works with organizations that recognize the importance of meaningful and impactful change. AK Events Group engages organizations by designing events that are creative, unique and unforgettable.

empowerment

AK Events Group supports organizations that empower those they serve by creating a platform to share and discuss important issues within today’s society. AK Events Group empowers the community by developing events that start conversations and debate.

AK Events Group has worked with the following organzations:

  • Houselink Community Homes
  • Starlight Children's Foundation
  • Float Valley
  • 360 Kids
