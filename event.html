    The AK Experience is designed to engage and empower young women. Join facilitated and exclusive curated experiences where you can expect to connect with like-minded individuals with similar interests and hobbies. Meeting once a week for four weeks, individuals will attend virtual workshops to create one-of-a-kind items and participate in thoughtful discussions.

    For $250.00 CAD, you get:

    • This experience includes access* to four virtually curated workshops, with all workshop materials shipped to attendees prior to the first workshop date.

    Attendees are not obligated to attend all workshops, but no refunds will be provided if you cannot attend. Workshops are not available individual purchases.

    *A zoom link will be provided to all registered attendees 24 hours in advance of each workshop

      @infinitesucculent

      Date: Thursday, January 14, 2020

      Time: 6:30pm - 7:00pm EST on Zoom

      Did you know houseplants are scientifically proven to reduce stress & anxiety, increase creavity & focus and enhance feelings of wellness?     Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I sign up alone?

    Yes of course. You can also encourage family and friends to purchase an AK Experience. The AK Experience package is a wonderful gift for loved ones.

    What do I need in order to participate?

    As part of your purchase, all workshop supplies will be delivered to your door in advance of the workshops. Ensure you have a device with internet connection (preferably a computer or tablet for the optimal experience).

    Do I need to purchase anything prior?

    No, all you have to do is show up!

    When will my package arrive?

    They will be shipped by Canada Post Regular Parcel from our headquarters in Toronto and a tracking number will be made available. Please allow at least 3-5 business days for your order to arrive. Unexpected delays may incur due to important safety measures implemented by Canada Post.

    What if I can't make it each week?

    We understand life gets busy, so if you can't make it just let us know.

    Who can sign up?

    Anyone can join us!

    How many people can attend the AK Experience?

    We find experience like these better when it's a small intimate group. We will limit this experience to 30 participants. Sign up quickly to save your spot!

    Are refunds available?

    Unfortunately, due to the nature of this package we are unable to provide any refunds after the purchase hase been made.
