    • AK Experience

    Try something new & get creative!

    The AK Experience is designed to engage and empower young women. Join facilitated and exclusive curated experiences where you can expect to connect with like-minded individuals with similar interests and hobbies.

    Meeting once a week for four weeks, individuals will attend virtual workshops to create one-of-a-kind items and participate in thoughtful discussions.

    For $250.00 CAD, you get:

    • All workshop materials shipped to you prior to the first workshop date
    • Online access to four curated workshops. A Zoom link that will be sent to you 24 hours in advance of each workshop)
      • Thursday, January 14 - Succulent Arrangement
      • Thursday, January 21 - Watercolour Painting
      • Thursday, January 28 - Paper Flower Arrangement
      • Thursday, February 4 - Embroidery

    You are not obligated to attend all workshops, but no refunds will be provided if you are unable to attend. Workshops are not available for individual purchase.

      • Succulent Arrangement with Rachael

      infinitesucculent

      Date: Thursday, January 14, 2021

      Time: 6:30pm - 7:30pm EST on Zoom

      Did you know houseplants are scientifically proven to reduce stress & anxiety, increase creativity & focus and enhance feelings of wellness? Engaging in creative activities is also proven to increase good health. So just imagine the double benefits you get when you get creative with the beauty and bounty of nature!

      In this workshop, Rachael will guide you as you create your own artful succulent arrangements to grace your interior spaces. Rachael will share tips for creating and tending to the living art, so the plants will continue to inspire for years to come!

      Rachael Cohen is an author, botanical stylist, environmental educator and energy worker, through her business Infinite Succulent. Rachael is dedicated to reconnecting to the infinite power of Nature, and our own infinite power, through creative and intentional engagement with the natural world. Rachael infuses all her art, styling and energy work services with Nature's life force energy and instills all she does with respect and love for Mother Earth.

      • Watercolour Painting with Tazeen

      tazsart

      Date: Thursday, January 21, 2021

      Time: 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm EST on Zoom

      TazsArt’s ‘Winter Wonderland Watercolour Night’ will walk participants through a creative, winter landscape themed watercolour painting scene. Participants are encouraged to customize their pieces as they see fit while going through some basic watercolour techniques such as blending, gradient wash, wet-on-wet painting and more. Keeping an open mind flowing through the artistic process is your strongest tool to completing a unique canvas composition!

      Tazeen is a multi-disciplinary visual artist based in Toronto, Canada. With a background in fine arts, marketing & interior design, she currently works in the luxury interior design/bespoke furniture industry. Tazeen continues to create on a freelance basis drawing inspiration from earth’s diverse landscapes (local and global). Her work in interior design contributes to her canvas compositions; focusing on colour, texture and symmetry.

      • Paper Flower Arrangement with Sara

      handmadebysarakim

      Date: Thursday, January 28, 2021

      Time: 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm EST on Zoom

      Let's make flowers! Join Sara as she teaches a step by step class on how to make standard paper flowers with regular card stock paper. This workshop will show you how to make standard paper flowers with different variations of them with no special tools or machines. All you need is just scissors, paper, and glue. You will also learn different ways to incorporate them into your wedding, events, and your home!

      Sara Kim the founder and artist behind Handmade By Sara Kim from Riverside, CA. Sara was a violin performance major and taught music for 10 years. Somewhere in between she needed a creative outlet and decided to craft as a hobby. Then on October 10, 2010, she made it "Facebook official" and launched her business Facebook page turning her hobby into a little online store where she sold a handful of things she made, paper flowers included. Sara loves everything DIY, making paper flowers for gifts and teaching them so you could make them for your loved ones also.

      • Embroidery with Quinn

      threadforfilth

      Date: Thursday, February 4, 2021

      Time: 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm EST on Zoom

      Join Quinn for a unique hands on experience! This workshop will be a complete walkthrough of how to create beautiful embroidery hoops and give attendees the building blocks to stitch their own hoops. This workshop is beginner-friendly, and you will leave with an adorable hoop to hang on your wall.

      Quinn Bray is an accomplished embroidery artist from Kelowna, BC. What started as stitching shapes into an old T-shirt became Threadforfilth Embroidery in 2017. Though her style of embroidery ranges, her favourite hoops to stitch revolve around immortalizing memories forever in thread. This includes stitching detailed pet portraits, wedding portraits, and house portraits, capturing moments to be kept forever. This is what brings her the joy her business thrives off of.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I sign up alone?

    Yes of course. You can also encourage family and friends to purchase an AK Experience. The AK Experience package is a wonderful gift for loved ones.

    What do I need in order to participate?

    As part of your purchase, all workshop supplies will be delivered to your door in advance of the workshops. Ensure you have a device with internet connection (preferably a computer or tablet for the optimal experience).

    Do I need to purchase anything prior?

    No, all you have to do is show up!

    When will my package arrive?

    They will be shipped by Canada Post Regular Parcel from our headquarters in Toronto and a tracking number will be made available. Please allow at least 3-5 business days for your order to arrive. Unexpected delays may incur due to important safety measures implemented by Canada Post.

    What if I can't make it each week?

    We understand life gets busy, so if you can't make it just let us know.

    Who can sign up?

    Anyone can join us!

    How many people can attend the AK Experience?

    We find experience like these better when it's a small intimate group. We will limit this experience to 30 participants. Sign up quickly to save your spot!

    Are refunds available?

    Unfortunately, due to the nature of this package we are unable to provide any refunds after the purchase has been made.
